Manali

Things about Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is a popular tourist destination known for its picturesque landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and lush green valleys. I had the opportunity to visit Manali last summer, and it was an experience that left an indelible mark on my memory.

The journey to Manali was an adventure in itself. As we made our way through the winding mountain roads, I was captivated by the breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks and the gushing rivers that flowed alongside the road. The fresh mountain air and the cool breeze provided much-needed relief from the scorching heat of the plains.

Goa

Things about Goa

Goa is the most popular destination for beachside holidays in India. It is the smallest state in the country by area, located on the southwestern coast. The region is blessed with beautiful scenic views, magnificent beaches, glorious natural landforms, tons of popular tourist attractions, and much more. It was also a major site of Portuguese colonial settlement in Asia

Jaipur

Things about Jaipur

Jaipur became known as “The Pink City” when, in 1876, Maharaja Ram Singh had most of the buildings painted pink—the color of hospitality—in preparation for a visit by Britain's Queen Victoria. Today, the city is known for its bazaars, forts, temples, palaces, and wildlife sanctuaries.
